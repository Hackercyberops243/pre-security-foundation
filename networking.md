# Networking Fundamentals

Networking allows devices to communicate and exchange data.

## Key Concepts

- Devices communicate using IP addresses
- Data is transmitted in packets
- Communication follows models like OSI and TCP/IP

## Important Components

- Router → connects networks  
- Switch → connects devices in a LAN  
- IP Address → identifies a device  

## Why It Matters in Cybersecurity

Most attacks happen over networks. Understanding traffic flow helps identify vulnerabilities and perform enumeration.
